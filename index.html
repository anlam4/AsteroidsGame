Welcome Space Traveler!

Click anywhere on the starry sky before beginning.
Press the left and right arrow keys to change direction.
Press the up arrow key to accelerate.
Press Shift to enter hyperspace (stops the spaceship and gives it a new position).
Press the spacebar to shoot bullets at asteroids.
Don't crash into the asteroids! After your shields are used up, your spaceship will no longer have protection against the asteroids.
Destroy all the asteroids to win the game.
