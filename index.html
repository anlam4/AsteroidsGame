Welcome Space Traveler!

Can you destroy all the asteroids before they destroy your spaceship?

Click anywhere on the starry sky before beginning.
Left and right arrow keys: change direction
Up arrow key: accelerate
Shift: enter hyperspace (stops the spaceship and gives it a new position)
Spacebar: shoot asteroid-destroying bullets

Don't crash into the asteroids! Once your shields are used up,
your spaceship will be vulnerable to those darn space rocks.
